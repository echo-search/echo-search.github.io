What is EchoSearch?

EchoSearch is a customizable search engine with themes, voice input, and extras.

Is EchoSearch free?

Yes. EchoSearch is completely free to use.

How do I change the theme?

Use the theme selector at the top of the homepage.

Does EchoSearch save my searches?

Search history is stored locally in your browser only.

Can I clear my history?

Yes. Use the Clear History button under the search list.

Can I use voice search?

Yes. Click the mic button and speak your query. However, this feature is only available on chronium-based browsers. Non-chronium based browsers (e.g. Safari) doesn't support this feature.

Visit the EchoSearch Defaulting page for links for Edge and Chrome.

Yes. EchoSearch is responsive and mobile-friendly.

No. It does not track or sell your data.
