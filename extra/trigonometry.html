Trigonometry Tutor & Quiz

Intro to Trigonometry

Trigonometry relates angles to side lengths in right triangles. The three main functions are:

  • sin(θ) = opposite / hypotenuse
  • cos(θ) = adjacent / hypotenuse
  • tan(θ) = opposite / adjacent

Unit Circle

The unit circle has radius 1. Coordinates (x, y) correspond to (cosθ, sinθ).
